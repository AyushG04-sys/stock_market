    Welcome to the Market Academy

    1. The Absolute Basics

    What is a stock? Buying a stock means buying a tiny piece of ownership in a real company.

    What is a portfolio? The collection of all the different stocks and cash you currently own.

    2. The Golden Rules of Trading

    • Buy Low, Sell High: Purchase stocks when they dip, and sell when they are overvalued.
    • Don't Panic Sell: The market goes up and down every day. Seeing red is normal.
    • Diversify: Don't put all your money into one single company. Spread your risk.
    • Do Your Research: Check the "Market News" tab to see why a stock is moving.

    3. Common Market Jargon

    • Bull Market: When the overall stock market is steadily rising.
    • Bear Market: When the overall market is falling and investors are scared.
    • Volatility: How wildly a stock's price jumps up and down.
    • Candlestick: The red and green blocks on your charts showing Open, High, Low, and Close prices.
